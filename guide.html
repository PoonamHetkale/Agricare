Crop Protection Guide

Rice

Common pests: Stem Borer, Leaf Folder.

Recommended pesticide: Chlorpyrifos.

Wheat

Common pests: Aphids.

Recommended pesticide: Imidacloprid.

Vegetables

Common pests: Caterpillars.

Recommended pesticide: Neem Oil Spray.
